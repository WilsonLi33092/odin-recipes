Lasgana

The best lasgana ever

Looking for a way to impress a whole party of people? Then look no further, this recipe will sweep them off their feet!

With a unique taste and tons of servings this is perfect for any gathering, or if you just for youtself then you'll have tons of leftovers!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion, and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasgana noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps to make lasgana

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasgana according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasgana rest before serving
