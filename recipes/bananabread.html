Banana Bread

Looking for a way to impress people with your baking skills? This recipe is the one for you!

With this easy to follow along recipe, you're sure to impress whoever you need with this amazing banana bread!

Ingreidents needed

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps to make banana bread

  1. Combine the dry ingreidents into one bowl
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed banana
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven
