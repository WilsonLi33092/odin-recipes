Pancakes

What's the most important meal of the day? Everyone says its breakfast so why not start your day with these amazing pancakes!

These pancakes are sure to make you crave for more!

Ingredients required

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and Butter
  • Egg

Steps to make pancakes!

  1. Sift the dry ingreidents together
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot pan
  4. Cook for a few minutes, then flip
  5. Continue cooking until golden brown on each side
